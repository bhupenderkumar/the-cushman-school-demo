Our Mission & History

The Cushman School was founded in 1967 by a group of visionary educators who shared a passion for providing a high-quality education in a safe and supportive environment. Our school has grown and evolved over the years, but our commitment to our core values remains unwavering.

At Cushman, we believe that teaching is not just about imparting knowledge, but about inspiring a love of learning that lasts a lifetime. Our experienced and dedicated faculty use innovative and engaging teaching methods to bring the curriculum to life, making learning fun and relevant for all students.

We believe that extracurricular activities and community service are essential components of a well-rounded education. From sports and clubs to volunteer opportunities, we offer a wide range of activities that foster teamwork, leadership, and social responsibility, helping our students become confident and compassionate individuals.
