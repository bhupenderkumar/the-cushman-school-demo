Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-4, emphasizing social-emotional development, literacy, and numeracy skills. Our experienced teachers create a nurturing environment that fosters curiosity and exploration.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program (Grades K-4), students delve into core subjects, including reading, writing, mathematics, science, and social studies. Our teachers use a balanced approach to instruction, incorporating project-based learning and hands-on activities.

📝

Middle/Upper

The Middle/Upper program (Grades 5-8) builds upon the foundations established in previous years, with a focus on advanced academic rigor, critical thinking, and creativity. Our students explore various electives and participate in leadership opportunities.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program offers a comprehensive curriculum in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, with hands-on projects and real-world applications. Students develop problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and collaboration.

🎭

Arts

The Cushman School's Arts program provides students with opportunities to explore their creativity through various mediums, including music, visual arts, drama, and dance. Our experienced instructors nurture artistic expression and appreciation.

🏋️‍♀️

Athletics

Our Athletics program fosters a love for physical activity, teamwork, and healthy competition. Students participate in various sports, including basketball, soccer, and volleyball, under the guidance of experienced coaches.
