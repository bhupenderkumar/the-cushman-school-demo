Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3 and 4, and is designed to foster a love of learning and a sense of community. With a focus on play-based learning and socialization, our Pre-K program lays the foundation for future academic success.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 4th grade, and is designed to build on the skills and knowledge gained in Pre-K. With a focus on reading, writing, math, and science, our Elementary program provides a strong foundation for future academic success.

🔬

Middle and Upper School

Our Middle and Upper School program serves students from 5th to 8th grade, and is designed to challenge students and prepare them for high school. With a focus on advanced coursework, extracurricular activities, and community service, our Middle and Upper School program helps students develop into confident, compassionate, and curious individuals.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in science, technology, engineering, and math. With hands-on learning experiences and project-based learning, our STEM program helps students develop problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and creativity.

🎨

Arts

Our Arts program is designed to provide students with a range of creative outlets, including music, art, drama, and dance. With a focus on self-expression and creativity, our Arts program helps students develop important life skills, including communication, collaboration, and critical thinking.

🏈

Athletics

Our Athletics program is designed to provide students with opportunities to develop physical skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship. With a focus on fair play, sportsmanship, and community engagement, our Athletics program helps students develop important life skills and build lasting relationships with their peers.
