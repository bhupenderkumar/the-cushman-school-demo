Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed to foster a love of learning in our youngest students, with a focus on play-based exploration and discovery. Our experienced teachers create a nurturing environment that promotes social, emotional, and cognitive development.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program, students engage in hands-on learning experiences that make academic concepts fun and accessible. Our experienced teachers use a range of teaching methods to cater to different learning styles and abilities.

📝

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper programs build on the foundation established in our Elementary program, with a focus on developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills. Our experienced teachers guide students as they explore academic subjects in depth and prepare for high school and beyond.

🧠

STEM

Our STEM program is designed to inspire students to explore the wonders of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Through hands-on projects and real-world applications, we help students develop problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and creativity.

🎭

Arts

Our Arts program is dedicated to fostering creativity and self-expression in our students. From music and drama to visual arts and dance, we offer a range of programs that help students develop their talents and interests.

🏋️‍♀️

Athletics

Our Athletics program is designed to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship in our students. From team sports to individual activities, we offer a range of programs that cater to different interests and abilities.
