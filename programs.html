Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for young learners who are eager to explore and discover the world around them. Our experienced teachers create a nurturing environment that fosters social, emotional, and cognitive growth.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program, students build upon their foundational skills and explore various subjects in-depth. Our small class sizes and dedicated teachers ensure that each student receives personalized attention and support.

🤔

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs cater to students who are transitioning to more complex academic challenges. Our expert faculty provides guidance and support as students navigate this critical phase of their educational journey.

🎤

STEM

The Cushman School offers a comprehensive STEM program that integrates science, technology, engineering, and math concepts into daily learning. Our students develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation skills through hands-on projects and experiments.

🎨

Arts

At The Cushman School, we believe that arts education is essential for a well-rounded education. Our Arts program offers a range of courses and activities that foster creativity, self-expression, and appreciation for the arts.

🏆

Athletics

Our Athletics program focuses on developing teamwork, sportsmanship, and physical fitness skills. Our students participate in various sports and activities, from basketball and soccer to track and field, under the guidance of experienced coaches.
